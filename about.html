Our Sourcing & Export Journey

TerraBric Global bridges high-purity raw clay minerals and sustainable biomass resources with modern builders and distributors worldwide.

Our Heritage

A decade of refining raw earth into world-class structural materials and clean energy.

Founded on a passion for geological craftsmanship and environmental balance, TerraBric began as a specialized regional clay works supplier. Recognizing the global demand for dependable, weather-resistant structural components and carbon-neutral fuels, we consolidated regional kilns and timber residue plants under a unified export brand.

Today, our client portfolio ranges from architectural firms in Western Europe to public housing projects in Oceania and clean-burning biomass power plants in Northeast Asia. We stand by a simple promise: consistent dimensional tolerance, maximum thermal mass retention, and low-waste manufacturing processes.

Milestones

A Decade of Growth

Key moments that shaped our journey from a local materials factory into an international supplier.

2011

Foundry Inception

Established our first modern brick-firing facility with high-capacity vacuum extrusion technology.

2015

First Export Line

Expanded operations to export flooring tiles and paving blocks to markets across Southeast Asia.

2018

Biomass Integration

Opened a dedicated wood pellet production plant, utilizing clean forestry residue to provide carbon-neutral energy solutions.

2022

ENplus A1 Certification

Received ENplus A1 certification for our wood pellets and ISO 9001 for our structural bricks, marking a step forward in quality assurance.

2026

Global Sourcing Network

Currently exporting over 120,000 tons of materials yearly, supporting architectural projects on 4 continents.

Quality & Logistics

Engineered for Maritime Transit

Moving heavy structural products and moisture-sensitive wood pellets across oceans requires rigorous protocols. Here is how we guarantee your shipment arrives intact:

🚢

FOB & CIF Shipping

We work directly with major shipping lines to secure reliable freight schedules. We manage all container bookings, custom declarations, and Bill of Lading documentation.

📦

Sealed Packaging

Our tiles and bricks are securely banded on heavy-duty wooden pallets, protected with thick styrofoam edging, and wrapped in industrial UV-resistant shrink-wrap.

🛡️

Quality Auditing

Every production batch undergoes compression strength, water absorption, and moisture audits. Certified inspect sheets accompany every container load.
